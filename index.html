Why We Started ATURE

In the heart of Vietnam’s Ha Tinh province, a young girl named Linh Dinh grew up surrounded by the quiet beauty of rural life. She watched as farmers toiled in the fields, their hands weaving stories into the earth. But as the years passed, those fields grew silent—families left for distant opportunities, leaving behind a fading tapestry of traditions. Inspired by her childhood and the Vietnamese government’s “One Commune, One Product” initiative, Linh founded ATURE to preserve these vanishing stories and connect small producers with the world, ensuring that the heartbeat of Vietnamese heritage continues to echo through authentic goods and heartfelt narratives.
